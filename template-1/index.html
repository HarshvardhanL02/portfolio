// where engineering meets software

Mechanical engineering and software aren't that different in how I think about them. Both involve understanding a system, finding where it breaks down, and designing something better. I'm drawn to automation because it sits exactly at that intersection.

Right now, that means writing tools. Eventually, I want to apply the same thinking to physical systems — control loops, mechatronics, and aerospace systems where precision actually matters.

⚙️ Automation & Control

Interested in how feedback systems work — from PID controllers to larger automated workflows. Exploring the software side while building mechanical intuition.

✈️ Aeronautical Technologies

Fascinated by flight systems and the precision engineering required. A long-term interest I plan to explore further in upper years.
